# Why Jin

Jin exists because FastAPI teams need business-level monitoring inside the app they already run.

## Why We Built It

- to catch business KPI drift before customers do
- to keep setup close to the FastAPI response model
- to let teams review issues without shipping data to a separate hosted tool
- to keep observations, baselines, and checks local to the project

## Where We Want To Go

- faster model-first setup
- simpler onboarding for new customer projects
- better issue review and explanation flows
- cleaner reports for product owners and leadership
- more automation around setup and change detection, without hiding the underlying data

## Product Promise

Jin should stay:

- local
- explainable
- model-first
- useful to product owners and engineers alike
